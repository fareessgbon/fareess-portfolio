## The brief

The Clinical Courier is a weekly educational newsletter for Kerecis sales
representatives. I created a sample issue to explore how clinical research,
training updates, and reminders could be presented more clearly.

## How it went

Structure first. I organized the newsletter into three repeatable sections:
Number of the Week, The Healing Edit, and Stay Up to Date.

Then the visuals. I used Figma, branded typography, imagery, and data
visualization to make the content easier to scan.

Then the research. I translated findings from a 465-patient study into a
simple comparison of average hospital length of stay.

<Callout kind="method"> The goal was not only to design one issue, but to create a structure that could support future newsletters. </Callout>

## What stuck with me

Small-client work is a masterclass in listening. The CEO knew the brand better
than I ever could. My job was building the instrument that let that knowledge
become a website.
